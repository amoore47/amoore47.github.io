Mane Focus is a scenic horse farm located on several acres in the heart of Virginia Beach. Our facilities include a twelve stall barn plus a four stall shedrow, round pen, and sand arena. We do offer pony birthday parties, including facilities to grill or serve cake, pictures with one of our resident ponies, and a short ride for each child. Please contact us for more information.

We do not currently offer boarding, but check back in the future for information on lessons or training opportunities.
